## Features Section

### Service Plans
Build complete orders of worship with drag-and-drop simplicity. Start from templates or create your own flow. Every song, reading, announcement, and transition—organized and ready.

### Team Coordination
Assign roles, schedule volunteers, and keep everyone on the same page. From worship leaders to tech teams, your people know exactly where to be and what to do.

### Song Library
Maintain your complete music repository with arrangements, keys, and notes. Search instantly, share with musicians, and build setlists in seconds.

### Roster Management
Schedule assignments across services and teams. Visual calendars, automatic reminders, and conflict detection keep your ministry running smoothly.
